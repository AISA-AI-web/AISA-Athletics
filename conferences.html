Athletics Conferences 2025-2026

Our Conferences

AISA Lions compete in four conferences spanning elementary, middle, JV, and varsity levels — connecting our student-athletes with peers across the UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, and Saudi Arabia.

Overview

Four Conferences. One Lion Pride.

From Grade 4 jamborees to Varsity championship tournaments across the Gulf, every level of AISA Athletics has a competitive home. Information on this page is sourced directly from the official 2025-2026 GAAC, ISAC, EAC, and JEAC handbooks.

GAAC

Gulf Athletics & Activities Conference · Founded 2024

GAAC is AISA's premier international conference, connecting American-curriculum schools across the UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, and Saudi Arabia. A High School Traveling Team championship conference, GAAC tournaments represent the highest level of competition on AISA's calendar with travel across the GCC. AISA and Dunecrest American School (DAS) joined as new members in 2025-2026.

ISAC

ISAC

International Schools Activities Conference · Founded 2024

ISAC is a four-school local conference based in Abu Dhabi, providing the foundation for regular-season league play at the Middle School, Junior Varsity, and Varsity levels. With frequent home-and-away matchups and reduced travel, ISAC is the primary competitive home for AISA's broadest pool of student-athletes — including the All-Star awards program at the close of each season.

Region Abu Dhabi (Local)
Levels MS (Blue) · JV · Varsity
Format Home/Away League + Playoffs
Travel Local Only

EAC

Emirates Athletics Conference · Middle School Level

EAC is a six-school Middle School league connecting American-curriculum schools across Abu Dhabi and Dubai. Each school plays a five-game league season, with the top four advancing to a one-day final tournament. EAC also operates a development league (DEAC) for less experienced student-athletes, ensuring every AISA Middle School Lion has a place to play.

Region UAE (Abu Dhabi & Dubai)
Level MS Blue (EAC) · MS Yellow (DEAC)
Format League + 4-Team Final Tournament
Travel Intra-UAE

JEAC

Junior Emirates Athletics Conference · Elementary Level

JEAC is the elementary version of EAC, designed for student-athletes in Grades 4-6. The focus is on fun, fundamental skill development, sportsmanship, and learning through game play. Grades 4-5 are entirely inclusive of all ability levels, while Grade 6 begins to introduce competitive concepts in preparation for the Middle School EAC program. JEAC events are held as jamborees rather than competitive tournaments, with no individual or team awards.

Region UAE (Abu Dhabi & Dubai)
Level Elementary (Grades 4-6)
Format Jamboree (No Awards)
Focus Fun · Skills · Sportsmanship
