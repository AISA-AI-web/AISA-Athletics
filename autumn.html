Autumn Season

The school year opens with energy on the volleyball court and in the pool. The Autumn season runs from September through November across all four AISA conferences.

Season Overview

Autumn Athletics at AISA

Autumn is "Season One" across GAAC, ISAC, and EAC. Volleyball is the headline sport at every level, with Swimming running as an Invitational across all three conferences. JEAC elementary Soccer also runs in this season, and GAAC's Leadership Conference rounds out the cultural calendar.

Volleyball

Boys & Girls · All Levels

Volleyball is the headline Autumn sport at AISA, with teams at every competitive level. Middle School and JV teams compete in the ISAC and EAC leagues, while Varsity competes at GAAC for the regional championship. Played by FIVB rules with GAAC-specific net heights for each level.

Varsity Boys Varsity Girls JV Boys JV Girls MS Boys MS Girls

Conferences: GAAC · ISAC · EAC
🏊

Swimming

Boys & Girls · MS / JV / Varsity

Swimming runs as an Invitational at all three high school conferences in the Autumn season. The competitive program follows FINA swimming rules. AISA swimmers can enter up to 4 individual events and 2 relays per meet, with morning and afternoon sessions for distance and stroke events.

Varsity Boys Varsity Girls JV Boys JV Girls MS Mixed

Conferences: GAAC · ISAC · EAC (all Invitational)
⚽

Cubs Soccer

Elementary · Grades 4-6

Cubs Soccer is the Autumn jamboree event for AISA's elementary student-athletes, run through the Junior Emirates Athletics Conference (JEAC). Played 7-a-side on a quarter-field with no offside rule and unlimited substitutions, weekly practices build fundamentals throughout September and October, with the focus on fun, skill development, and equal playing time for every athlete, culminating in the JEAC Final Matches against regional schools.

Grades 4-5 Mixed Grade 6 Boys Grade 6 Girls

Conference: JEAC (Jamboree, no awards)
🎯

Leadership Conference

GAAC Invitational

The GAAC Leadership Conference is a non-athletic Invitational event held during the Autumn season. AISA student leaders join peers from across the Gulf region for a multi-day program of workshops, speakers, collaborative activities, and cross-cultural exchange.

Varsity Delegation

Conference: GAAC (Invitational · Up to 10 students)
